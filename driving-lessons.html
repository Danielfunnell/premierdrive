The Car

The tuition car is a dual controlled new shape Ford Fiesta, a small hatchback on the outside making it very easy to drive and manoeuvre, but with a spacious interior full of features designed to give a great level of driver comfort while your learning to drive, such as: Power steering | Air-conditioning | Height adjustable drivers seat and steering column | Drivers airbag | Electric windows and Door mirror adjuster | Dual controls.

Driving Lessons

Your lessons will be structured and designed to suit your skill level, whether it is your first time driving or if you are an experienced learner. Every driving lesson is recorded and monitored in your drivers record, which will be given to you on your first lesson. The drivers record is a syllabus designed to help you and your driving instructor keep a record of your progress while you are learning to drive, basically it lets you know where you stand. The duration and regularity of your driving lessons depends on you, however we recommend a 2 hour lesson once a week to keep you learning at a good pace

Intensive courses

Premier Drive’s intensive Driving courses are quite simply designed to get you through your test quicker. They are recommended for people who don’t have much spare time but want to learn hard during a week’s holiday that they have coming up etc. They can be tailor made to suit your requirements and availability. Intensive courses are usually done over a week and semi-intensive over 2-3 weeks for those of you with a bit more time. The number of hours you require depends on your current standard. Find out more about our intensive driving courses and prices.

Pass Plus

This is a course for newly qualified drivers. It consists of at least 6 hours advanced training in these areas:

  • Motorways
  • Town driving
  • Country driving
  • All weather driving
  • Night driving

By improving your driving skills and hazard awareness you can reduce the risk of being involved in a serious crash. Many insurance companies offer a substantial discount for young drivers who have completed a Pass Plus course within 12 months of passing their test.

Motorway Lessons

These driving lessons are designed for drivers that have just passed their test to give you professional instruction and invaluable experience on these high-speed potentially dangerous roads.

Refresher Lessons

Designed for drivers that have passed their test but have not been behind the wheel for sometime. Also for drivers that hold a European licence but want tuition and experience on the British roads.
