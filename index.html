Our Courses

Weekly Driving Lessons

Weekly structured pay as you go driving lessons available in hour, 1.5 hour or 2 hour slots. Every lesson is recorded and monitored in your drivers record and emailed to you after each session.

Semi Intensive Driving Course

Taliored made semi intensive driving course, normally completed over 2-4 weeks for those of you with a bit more time.

Intensive Driving Courses

1-2 week intensive driving courses designed for beginners, intermediates and advanced pupils with the driving test at the end. Assessment lessons from just £20 to find the right course for you.

Motorway Lessons

Designed for new or nervous drivers to give you professional instruction and invaluable experience on these high speed potentially dangerous roads. Can be done in instructors or own car.

Pass Plus

This 6 hour course is for newly qualified drivers with advanced training on motorways, country roads, town driving, all weather driving and night driving which may lead to potential insurance discounts.

Refresher Lessons

Designed for drivers that have passed their test but have not been behind the wheel for sometime. Also for drivers that hold a european licence but want experience on British roads.
